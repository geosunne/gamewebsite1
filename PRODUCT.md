# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

BTW game serves casual players, children, and students who want quick browser games without downloads, accounts, or setup. They arrive with short attention spans, often from search or shared links, and need to identify something fun fast.

## Product Purpose

BTW game is a public game portal for discovering and playing free online games instantly. Success means visitors trust the site, find a game quickly, open game pages without friction, and return for more casual play.

## Brand Personality

Fast, playful, trustworthy. The site should feel energetic and easy to scan, with enough color and motion to feel game-like while staying clear, safe, and credible for younger players and students.

## Anti-references

Avoid casino or betting visual language, including dark neon, jackpot cues, slot-machine energy, aggressive red-and-gold urgency, and misleading reward patterns. Avoid overly childish preschool styling, toy-like clutter, and mascot-heavy UI. Avoid dark esports aesthetics, heavy black backgrounds, smoky gradients, and hostile shooter-lobby tone.

## Design Principles

Move players to games quickly. Make browsing, search, and game cards immediately understandable with visible titles, useful grouping, and low-friction navigation.

Keep playfulness controlled. Use color, rhythm, thumbnails, and small motion to make the site feel alive without compromising readability or trust.

Earn trust before clicks. Present game pages with clear metadata, predictable controls, stable layout, and no deceptive urgency.

Design for shared devices. Keep layouts responsive, tap targets generous, and copy simple enough for children and students while still feeling credible to adults.

Let games carry variety. Use game thumbnails and categories as the main visual diversity, not unrelated decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus visibility, keyboard navigation, and text legibility. Support reduced motion. Avoid relying on color alone for state. Keep mobile interaction comfortable for touch and readable on small screens.
