About Tomb Runner

Tomb Runner is an exhilarating 3D endless running game that takes you on a thrilling adventure through ancient ruins and treacherous paths. Playing as a brave explorer, your objective is to run as far as possible while navigating a dangerous, crumbling temple environment. The game tests your reflexes as you sprint across narrow stone bridges, dodge deadly traps, and slide under massive obstacles. Along the way, you must collect shiny diamond-shaped coins and precious gems, which can be used to unlock a fun cast of characters, including the daring Lara Bones. With its fast-paced gameplay, dynamic camera angles, and progressively increasing speed, Tomb Runner offers an addictive challenge for players seeking to beat their high scores. Survive the ancient traps, collect the lost treasures, and see how far you can run in this action-packed arcade experience!
