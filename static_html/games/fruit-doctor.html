But forget about human patients—in this clinic, you're treating a colorful cast of injured fruits! From strawberries needing internal seed removal to watermelons requiring emergency stitches, every patient presents a quirky medical challenge. You'll have access to an entire arsenal of professional medical tools, including scalpels, lasers, tweezers, and specialized injectors. The game stands out with its incredible attention to detail and high-quality sound design, providing a relaxing ASMR experience as you cut, clean, and heal your fruity friends. With hundreds of levels and a progressive reward system to upgrade your clinic,Fruit Doctor offers a perfect blend of creativity, humor, and stress relief for players of all ages.
