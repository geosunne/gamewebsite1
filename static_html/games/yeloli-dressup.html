About Yeloli Dress Up

Welcome to Yeloli Dressup, an enchanting 3D fashion game that perfectly combines magical styling with a relaxing idle garden mechanic! Based on the beloved anime series, this game allows you to interact with your favorite Yeloli fairies and fulfill your ultimate dress-up dreams. The core of your magical journey begins in the flower garden. By unlocking and upgrading beautiful flower beds, you will automatically generate a steady stream of gold coins over time. Harvest this idle wealth and head straight to the massive wardrobe! Spend your hard-earned coins to collect thousands of gorgeous dresses, elegant shoes, and sparkling fairy wings. Use your creativity to mix and match these stunning items and style your Yeloli fairy to perfection. Whether you enjoy managing a growing coin empire or designing breathtaking fairy outfits, Yeloli Dressup offers a visually stunning and deeply relaxing experience for all fashion lovers! In WC Waiting Line, your mission is to clear the entire queue by matching colors. At the top of the screen, you'll see a row of toilet stalls, each with a colored roll of toilet paper above it. Below, characters of various colors are waiting in line. Tap on the character at the front of the line to move them into an empty stall. If the character's color matches the toilet paper color of that stall, they will be cleared instantly, earning you points and freeing up the stall. If the colors don't match, the character will stay in the stall, taking up a valuable slot. You must analyze the upcoming colors in the queue and match them strategically with the paper available. WC Waiting Line is a clever and addictive 2D puzzle game that puts your logic and color-matching skills to the test in a hilarious everyday scenario. The game features a crowded line of people waiting for the restroom, each wearing a specific color. Your goal is to tap them into the available toilet stalls at the top. However, there's a catch: a character will only leave the stall (be cleared) if their color matches the toilet paper currently assigned to that stall.
