In the Battle Phase, you deploy your troops into intense real-time combat where they automatically engage enemies. Success requires strategic planning—balancing your army's health and damage while using special items like food shields. With its vibrant 3D cartoon graphics, smooth animations, and a steady progression system, Ant War offers a unique perspective on tactical warfare.
