About Santa Licious

Get into the holiday spirit with Santalicious, a delightful and addictive line-matching puzzle game perfect for the Christmas season. In this cozy winter adventure, players help a cheerful Santa Claus gather various sweets, including heart-shaped lollipops, stars, and classic hard candies. The core gameplay revolves around drawing lines to connect three or more identical adjacent candies to clear them from the board. Unlike traditional swap-based match-3 games, Santalicious rewards strategic pathfinding; creating long chains is key to success. Chains of six or more candies grant powerful bonus items, while massive chains of seven or more award precious extra time to help you clear the stage. With its vibrant 2D cartoon graphics, soothing holiday soundtrack, and hundreds of levels with unique objectives, Santalicious offers a sweet and relaxing challenge for players of all ages.
