About Merge Number Puzzle

Merge Number Puzzle is an innovative and highly addictive brain-teaser that puts a unique mathematical twist on the classic merge genre. Instead of sliding or drawing lines, players are given a set of number cards at the bottom of the screen (such as +1, +2, or -1) and a grid filled with numbered blocks. Your goal is to tap a block on the grid to add the bottom card's value to it. If the newly calculated number matches the adjacent blocks, they will instantly merge into a single block with a value of +1! This clever combination of basic math and spatial strategy requires you to think several moves ahead. With its vibrant graphics, unlimited tactical possibilities, and highly satisfying chain reactions, Merge Number Puzzle is the perfect stress-relieving game for players who love to sharpen their logic and math skills.
