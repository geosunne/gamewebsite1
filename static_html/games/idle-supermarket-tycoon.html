Take charge of every aspect of the business: stock up on various goods starting from the agricultural product zone, manage cashiers to reduce checkout lines, and expand your parking lot to welcome a massive influx of customers. As an idle game, your supermarket never sleeps; it continues to generate a steady stream of passive income even when you are offline! Reinvest your earnings wisely to upgrade product quality, hire efficient managers, and eventually unlock larger, higher-star supermarket buildings. With its charming 3D isometric graphics and in-depth management mechanics, this tycoon game will test your entrepreneurial skills. Are you ready to become a billionaire?
