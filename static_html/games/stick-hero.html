About Stick Hero

Stick Hero is an addictive puzzle and strategy game that combines classic stickman themes with engaging math logic mechanics. In this captivating 2D cartoon adventure, players control a brave stickman hero tasked with climbing towers filled with dangerous monsters, shiny treasures, and captive princesses. Your power is represented by a number above your head. To survive and conquer, you must strategically drag your hero to attack enemies with lower numbers, absorbing their power to grow stronger. As you progress, the towers become more complex, requiring careful calculation and logical thinking to determine the correct order of attacks. Collect powerful swords, unlock epic skins, and fight massive bosses in the arena. Perfect for players who love brain-teasing puzzles combined with RPG elements, Stick Hero offers endless hours of strategic fun and decompression.
