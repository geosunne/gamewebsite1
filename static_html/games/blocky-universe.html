About Blocky Universe

Blocky Universe is an engaging action-adventure game that combines the charm of voxel aesthetics with deep RPG progression. Set in a vast, fragmented galaxy, you take on the role of a brave explorer stranded on unknown planets. Your primary mission is to survive and thrive by gathering essential resources such as wood and rare gemstones. As you traverse through beautifully crafted blocky landscapes, you will encounter various hostile creatures that challenge your combat skills. By defeating enemies and completing quests, you earn gold and experience to upgrade your gear and abilities. The ultimate goal is to find and fix the ancient portals scattered across the universe, allowing you to travel to increasingly difficult and rewarding realms. Whether you enjoy the peaceful rhythm of resource collection or the adrenaline of monster hunting, Blocky Universe offers a rich and satisfying gameplay experience.
