About Craftify Car

\\\"Craftify Car\\\" is an imaginative and addictive simulation game that combines the joy of building with the thrill of racing. Set in a vibrant, 3D voxel world reminiscent of \\\"Minecraft,\\\" this game lets you become a master mechanic. Before each race, you enter the crafting grid, where you must strategically place chassis parts, wheels, and special accessories like propellers or drills. Once your masterpiece is built, you hit the track to race against an opponent. The tracks are filled with steep hills, gaps, and obstacles that test the physical balance and power of your creation. If your car is too front-heavy or lacks a boost, you might crash or get stuck! Win races to earn coins, unlock new blocks, increase your building capacity, and create the ultimate racing machine. It's a perfect blend of creativity and action for players of all ages. Start by tapping the \\\"Start Game\\\" button on the main menu. On the screen, you will see groups of fruit floating in the center. Your knife is positioned at the bottom of the screen. Tap the screen to shoot the knife upwards. Your goal is to hit as many fruits as possible. When a fruit is hit, it will be sliced into pieces and automatically fall into the blender at the bottom left. Once the blender is full, you complete the level. Be careful with your timing; missing the fruit doesn't end the game, but hitting multiple fruits in one throw can grant bonus points and coins! Keep an eye on the coin icons to increase your wealth and unlock new gear. Tap/Click: Fire the knife upwards from the bottom of the screen.
