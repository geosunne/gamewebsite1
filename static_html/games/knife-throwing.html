About Knife Throwing

Get ready for a fresh and challenging take on the classic knife-throwing game! In this hyper-casual arcade experience, you don't just throw knives straight ahead. Instead, your knife is stuck in a spinning target, swinging back and forth like a pendulum. A large arrow indicates the direction it will fly. Your goal is to tap the screen at the precise moment to launch the knife towards the next target above you. Timing is everything! If you tap too early or too late, your knife will miss the target, hit a brick wall, or fly off the screen, ending your run. The game is an endless climb to see how high you can go and how many targets you can hit in a row. With its minimalist 2D graphics and highly addictive \\\"just one more try\\\" gameplay, it's the perfect game to test your reflexes and kill some time.
