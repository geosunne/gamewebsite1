About Fruit Sort Master

Welcome to Fruit Sort Master, a delightful and addictive puzzle game where you assemble delicious fruit platters! In this game, your goal is to drag fruit slices from the bottom queue onto the grid. Once a platter is full, it clears from the board, awarding you points and filling your progress bar. As you advance through levels, you will unlock new and exotic fruits like grapefruits and pomelos. The game features a vibrant, cartoonish art style and relaxing gameplay perfect for killing time. Use helpful power-ups like the fruit cannon and tongs to manage your grid and strategize your moves.
