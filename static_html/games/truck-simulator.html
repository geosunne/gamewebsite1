About Truck Simulator

Truck Simulator is a challenging 2D physics-based driving game that puts your coordination and strategic thinking to the test. In this game, you take on the role of a truck driver tasked with navigating through treacherous mountain paths. The core of the gameplay revolves around balancing speed and fuel efficiency; go too fast and you might flip or burn through your gas, go too slow and you won't make it up the next steep incline. Before each level, you can visit the garage to upgrade four essential components: Speed (Engine), Petrol Tank, Wheels, and Suspension. Using the cash collected during your runs, you can turn a basic truck into a hill-climbing beast.
