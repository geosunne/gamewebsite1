About Super Brain

Super Brain is a delightful and addictive casual puzzle game that puts your observation and logic skills to the test. Set in a colorful 2D cartoon world, players are challenged to clear layers of cute, stacked animal shapes. To do this, you must carefully click on the top-menu darts that match the colors of the balloons attached to the topmost shapes. As you pop the balloons, the shapes fly away, revealing the hidden layers underneath. With its simple yet strategic gameplay, Super Brain offers a perfect balance of relaxation and mental stimulation. The difficulty gradually increases as the stacks become more complex, requiring careful planning to avoid getting stuck. Whether you are looking for a quick stress-relief session or a fun way to train your brain, this charming game provides endless hours of entertaining puzzle-solving.
