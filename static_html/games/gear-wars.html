About Gear Wars

Welcome to Gear Wars, a highly innovative strategy and merge game where mechanical gears determine the fate of your army! In this thrilling base-versus-base battle, your objective is to build a powerful gear engine to produce warrior units and crush the enemy tribe. The core gameplay revolves around placing and connecting different gears on a grid. You use Multiplier Gears to drive Warrior Gears. The higher the multiplier, the faster your warriors spawn! Merge two identical multiplier gears to upgrade them (for example, Level 2 + Level 2 = Level 4), drastically reducing the rotations needed to deploy troops. Connect multiple Warrior Gears to a single high-level Multiplier Gear to unleash a massive horde simultaneously. Unlock diverse warrior classes, upgrade your gear engine, and outsmart your opponents in this addictive tactical warfare!
