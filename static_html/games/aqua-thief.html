About Aqua Thief

Dive into a vibrant world of underwater crime and clever puzzles with Aqua Thief! In this engaging 2D action-puzzle game, you take on the role of a nimble yellow fish on a mission to collect precious gold coins scattered across the ocean floor. The core gameplay features an intuitive drawing mechanic: you must trace a continuous path for your character to follow. However, the waters are far from safe! Relentless police fish in blue suits patrol the area and will chase you the moment you enter their line of sight. Your goal is to strategically plan your movements to clear every coin and reach the exit without being caught. As you progress through hundreds of levels, the environments become more complex with moving obstacles and multiple guards. With its charming cartoon visuals, smooth touch controls, and satisfying logic challenges, Aqua Thief offers endless fun for players of all ages looking for a fresh spin on the puzzle genre.
