About Train Shooting

Train Shooting is an innovative and highly addictive casual idle game that perfectly blends the popular merge mechanic with thrilling shooting action. Players take command of an armed train moving continuously along a track, tasked with defending against relentless waves of bizarre monsters. To increase your firepower, you must spend coins to purchase new train carriages and merge two identical ones together to create a higher-level, more powerful unit. As you progress, you can unlock devastating new train skins, automatic wingman drones, and invest in powerful buffs to maximize your damage output. The game features an engaging progression system where you continuously upgrade your train's capacity, merge level, attack damage, and coin income. With its vibrant 3D cartoon graphics, seamless idle mechanics, and satisfying progression, Train Shooting provides endless entertainment for fans of merge and defense games.
