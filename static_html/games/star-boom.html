About Star Boom

Star Boom is a delightful and highly addictive casual puzzle game that brings the beloved classic \\\"Pop Star\\\" elimination gameplay to your screen. In this vibrant 2D game, players are presented with a grid filled with colorful, cute, jelly-like blocks. The objective is simple yet requires strategic thinking: tap on any group of two or more adjacent blocks of the same color to make them burst! The more blocks you pop in a single tap, the higher your score will be. Each level features a specific target score you must reach to progress to the next stage. To help you overcome tricky situations, the game offers handy power-ups like the Hammer to smash single blocks or a Magic Wand to change a block's color. With its cheerful graphics, satisfying popping effects, and endless levels, Star Boom is the perfect stress-relief game for players of all ages.
