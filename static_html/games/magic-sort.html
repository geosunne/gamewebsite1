About Magic Sort

Magic Sort is a captivating and brain-teasing puzzle game that puts your organizational skills to the ultimate test. In this vibrant world of magic potions, you are presented with several glass jars filled with layers of multi-colored liquids. Your mission is simple but challenging: sort the colors until each jar holds only one distinct hue. As you progress through hundreds of levels, the game introduces hidden layers marked with question marks, adding a layer of mystery and strategy to your every move. With its serene background, smooth pouring animations, and satisfying visual feedback, Magic Sort offers a perfect blend of mental exercise and relaxation. It’s an ideal game for players who enjoy logical challenges and want to improve their problem-solving skills in a stress-free environment.
