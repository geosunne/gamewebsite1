About Draw Bridge

Draw Bridge is a minimalist and intellectually stimulating 2D puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning and understanding of physics. In this game, your primary objective is to guide a small car across treacherous gaps to reach the finish flag safely. However, there are no pre-built bridges! You must use your drawing skills to create a sturdy path that can withstand the weight of the vehicle and the forces of gravity. From snowy cliffs to complex industrial zones filled with rotating obstacles, each level presents a unique engineering challenge. The game features a clean, vector-based cartoon art style that keeps the focus on the puzzle-solving experience. With hundreds of levels ranging from simple crossings to complex multi-step maneuvers, Draw Bridge offers a satisfying \\\"Aha!\\\" moment with every successful bridge you design. It’s the perfect game for players who enjoy quick, creative brain exercises.
