About Doomsday Ark

Doomsday Ark is an engaging strategic survival game set in a post-apocalyptic world where the land has been swallowed by the ocean. As a commander of a floating refuge, you must balance the daily management of your ark with intense defensive battles. The game features a dual-core experience: in the management phase, you assign survivors to produce food, craft weapons like bows, and gather essential materials. When the sun sets, you transition into a thrilling Roguelike defense mode. Here, you must deploy turrets and use powerful weapons to fend off hordes of swimming zombies. With every level gained during combat, you can choose from randomized upgrades to strengthen your arsenal. Featuring a charming Q-version art style and deep progression systems, Doomsday Ark offers a perfect mix of relaxing tycoon mechanics and exciting tactical combat.
