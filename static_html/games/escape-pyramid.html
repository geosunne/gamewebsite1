Escape Pyramid is a high-flying 3D hyper-casual runner that redefines the crowd-running genre with its unique vertical stacking mechanic. Starting as a lone runner, you must strategically pick up teammates to grow your tower taller, allowing you to bypass gaps and reach higher bonus multipliers at the end of each level. The game is set in a vibrant, minimalist world where the sky is the limit. Along the way, you’ll collect gold coins to visit the shop and unlock a variety of skins, transforming your squad into iconic superheroes. With its smooth physics, challenging level design, and the constant thrill of keeping your tower from toppling, Escape Pyramid offers a fresh and addictive experience for fans of innovative parkour games.
