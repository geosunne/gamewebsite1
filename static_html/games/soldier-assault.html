Welcome to Soldier Assault, an action-packed 3D hyper-casual runner that brilliantly combines thrilling shooting mechanics with brain-teasing math choices! In this addictive arcade game, you command a squad of brave stickman soldiers sprinting down a dangerous runway. Ahead of you looms a terrifying giant boss that you must defeat. To succeed, you have to steer your squad through the best mathematical gates (like +5 or x2) to multiply your forces while actively dodging negative gates and deadly traps. As your army grows, they automatically unleash a hail of bullets onto the retreating boss. Destroy the boss's health bar before reaching the finish line to claim victory! Collect cash and coins along the way to upgrade your soldiers' starting count and weapon damage. With its colorful 3D graphics, intuitive controls, and satisfying progression, Soldier Assault offers endless fun.
