About Star Tetris

Star Tetris is a visually captivating hyper-casual game set against a beautiful neon space background. Despite its name, this isn't your traditional block-stacking game; instead, it is an engaging reflex and timing challenge. Players control a glowing orb that glides along paths, and with a simple tap, you can make the orb jump to the opposite line. The main objective is to navigate through these geometric cosmic paths to collect all the floating yellow diamonds. However, space is full of dangers! You must carefully time your jumps to avoid hitting the deadly red obstacles. Successfully collecting all the gems in a level unlocks stunning star map fragments, eventually revealing beautiful constellations like Aries. With its minimalist 2D neon graphics, intuitive one-tap controls, and progressively challenging levels, Star Tetris offers a relaxing yet thrilling experience that perfectly tests your reflexes and concentration.
