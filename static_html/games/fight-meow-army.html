About Fight! Meow Army!

Fight Meow Army is a captivating 2D lane-defense strategy game that combines the charm of adorable felines with the intensity of tactical warfare. Starting in the primitive Stone Age Civilization, players must lead an army of Meow warriors to conquer enemy territory. The gameplay revolves around strategic resource management; you collect \\\"Fish\\\" as your primary currency to summon various cat soldiers, each with unique abilities, such as the humble pitchfork-wielding Farmer Meow. As you progress through the levels, you can unlock new civilizations, upgrade your units through a comprehensive card collection system, and face increasingly powerful feline foes. The game features a clean, hand-drawn art style that makes watching the chaotic cat battles both fun and visually satisfying. Whether you are managing your base or pushing toward the enemy portal, every decision counts in this epic meow-filled journey across history.
