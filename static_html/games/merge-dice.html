About Merge Dice

Merge Dice is a captivating and highly addictive puzzle game that brilliantly combines the strategic matching of a match-3 game with the satisfying progression of a 2048-style number puzzle. Set on a wooden 5x5 grid, your objective is to strategically place randomized colored dice blocks. When three or more dice with the exact same number of pips (dots) are placed adjacent to each other, they magically merge into a single die of the next higher value. For instance, three 2-dot dice will merge into one 3-dot die. The ultimate challenge is to reach the highest possible numbers and clear the board before it fills up entirely. With its colorful aesthetics, soothing wooden board design, and mentally stimulating gameplay loop, Merge Dice provides endless hours of relaxing yet challenging brain-training entertainment.
