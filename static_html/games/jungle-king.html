About Jungle King

Welcome to Jungle King, a charming and highly addictive idle RPG where you guide an adorable mushroom hero on an endless adventure through a magical forest! In this relaxing yet engaging game, your little mushroom automatically battles waves of forest creatures like giant bees and bugs. As you defeat enemies, you earn gold coins which can be used to continuously upgrade your core stats, such as Attack, HP, and Attack Speed. The journey doesn't stop there! As you progress and clear more stages, you will unlock exciting deep-progression features. Visit the store to summon powerful equipment, learn devastating skills, and hatch cute pets to fight alongside you. With its continuous progression system, your hero keeps growing stronger even when you are just watching. Dive into the beautiful 2D cartoon world of Jungle King and build the ultimate mushroom warrior!
