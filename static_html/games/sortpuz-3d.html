About SortPuz 3D

Welcome to SortPuz 3D, a highly addictive and visually pleasing water sort puzzle game designed to train your brain and soothe your mind. Your objective is simple yet challenging: sort various colored liquids mixed in different glass bottles until each bottle contains only one solid color. As you progress through the hundreds of levels, the puzzles become increasingly complex, requiring careful planning and logical thinking. The game features smooth pouring animations, a relaxing ASMR-like sound design, and a vibrant pastel aesthetic. Beyond the puzzles, you can collect coins to unlock a wide variety of stylish bottle shapes, beautiful background themes, and even unique avatars. Compete with players worldwide on the leaderboard and prove your sorting skills. Whether you need a quick mental workout or a relaxing escape, SortPuz 3D is the perfect casual puzzle experience!
