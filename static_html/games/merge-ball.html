About Merge Ball

Merge Ball is a brilliantly designed logic puzzle game that combines the satisfying feel of classic \\\"tap-to-clear\\\" mechanics with a strategic number evolution system. Set on a grid filled with colorful numbered spheres, your goal is to find groups of identical balls that are touching each other. When you tap on a ball within a connected group, all adjacent identical balls disappear, and the specific ball you clicked increases its value by one. This unique mechanic creates a dynamic environment: as balls are cleared, the spheres above them fall to fill the gaps, constantly shifting the layout and creating new tactical possibilities. It’s a game of foresight and space management. Can you clear the board efficiently and evolve your numbers to reach the ultimate goal? Dive into Merge Ball for a stress-relieving yet mentally stimulating experience.
