About Helix Jump

Helix Jump is a casual game with simple and addictively fun mechanics. In the game, you guide a bouncing ball continuously down a series of circular platforms until you crash. Fall through the cracks and avoid landing in the forbidden zones! How To Play Helix Jump The goal of Helix Jump is to guide a bouncing ball down through a series of rotating platforms without falling off the edge or hitting forbidden sections. The basic mechanics are easy to follow. The key is mastering your reflexes and looking ahead. To guide the bouncing ball, rotate the platforms to align the ball with the cracks. Do this in quick succession for a combo move and higher points. If you go through 3 levels in quick succession, you're invincible for the first bounce after. Note that Helix Jump features an endless number of levels with incremental difficulty, so you may want to pace yourself. The game has missions, visible on the left side of your screen, which give you coins when completed. Use the coins you earn via the missions and the game progression to unlock special skins for your ball: there are 27 skins available, some are common, some are rare, and the least common ones are funky! Power-ups in Helix Jump Helix Jump contains two main power-ups: Fireball mode: makes your ball invincible for the first bounce after passing through 3 or more consecutive platform gaps. When in "Fireball mode", your ball can safely touch even the forbidden color and will destroy the platform it lands on. Green Arrows: these are randomly placed on some of the platforms and can transform the ball via the "fireball mode" Helix Jump - The Original This is the official Helix Jump game developed by Voodoo. The game was initially released in 2018 on mobile devices and achieved viral success. You can now play this addictive game in your web browser for free at CrazyGames!
