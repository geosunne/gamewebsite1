About Stack Clash

Stack Clash is an engaging hyper-casual 3D strategy game that blends fast-paced resource collection with light base-building and tower defense elements. You control a dynamic stickman hero tasked with conquering a vibrant arena. Your primary objective is to run around the battlefield, gathering scattered bricks. Once collected, you must rush back to your designated base area to deposit them. These bricks automatically stack up to construct defensive towers and weapon emplacements. As your base grows stronger, you will face off against enemy stickmen who are trying to do the same. You need to balance the risk of venturing out for resources with the safety of your expanding fortress. With its colorful graphics, simple controls, and satisfying progression loop, Stack Clash offers a highly addictive experience for players who enjoy casual tactical gameplay.
