About Balls and Ropes

Welcome to the satisfying world of Balls and Ropes, where physics meets idle strategy! In this captivating 2D game, your mission is to build a complex system of bouncy ropes to capture and direct falling balls into a collection pipe. Every time a ball hits a rope, you earn cash. The game features a unique progression system: start by placing basic ropes and then use the \\\"Synthesize\\\" mechanic to merge identical ropes into more powerful, higher-earning versions. As your income grows, you can purchase \\\"New Balls\\\" to increase the frequency and volume of your earnings. Each level presents a new target income goal to reach. With its vibrant colors, smooth animations, and the rhythmic sound of balls bouncing, Balls and Ropes offers a relaxing yet addictive experience that keeps you coming back for more. Can you optimize your rope layout and reach the highest level? Addictive Timing Gameplay: Master the rhythm of the swinging claw to grab the most valuable treasures with precision.\ \ Strategic Shop System: Spend your hard-earned gold on items like dynamite and strength potions to clear levels more efficiently.\ \ Diverse Underground Treasures: Discover everything from tiny gold nuggets to high-value diamonds and unpredictable mystery bags.\ \ Challenging Obstacles: Avoid heavy rocks and obstacles that slow down your mining progress and waste precious time.\ \ Progressive Difficulty: Each level increases the target score, forcing you to plan your grabs more carefully. To begin, simply tap the screen when the swinging claw is aimed at a target you want to retrieve. The claw will extend and grab whatever it hits first. Larger items like big gold nuggets are worth more but take longer to pull up. Diamonds are small and fast but extremely valuable. If you accidentally grab a heavy rock, you can use a dynamite stick (if you have one) to destroy it and save time. Your goal is to reach the \\\"Target\\\" score shown at the top of the screen before the \\\"Time\\\" reaches zero. Success moves you to the next stage, while failure ends your mining career. Use the shop between levels to prepare for tougher challenges! Simply tap or click anywhere on the screen to launch the claw. Tap the dynamite icon while reeling in an item to use it. Use strength potions to speed up the retrieval of heavy, valuable gold. \ \ Q: Does my gold carry over between levels? \ A: Yes, your total score accumulates throughout the game, allowing you to afford more expensive items in the shop.
