About Jelly Merge

Welcome to Jelly Merge, a brilliantly designed puzzle game that will put your spatial logic and strategic thinking to the test! In this vibrant and colorful brain-teaser, your objective is to merge all patches of liquid jelly that share the same color. By swiping up, down, left, or right, you command all jellies on the board to slide simultaneously until they hit a wall or another object. When two jellies of the same color collide, they merge into one! As you progress, the game introduces highly complex terrains, labyrinth-like maps, and multiple colors that you must manage at once. You will need to cleverly use the environment's corners and obstacles to block certain jellies while moving others into the perfect position. With its satisfying jiggly animations, stress-relieving mechanics, and progressively challenging levels, Jelly Merge is the ultimate puzzle experience!
