About Makeup Beauty

Makeup Beauty is an incredibly satisfying and relaxing ASMR makeover game that lets you step into the role of a professional beauty stylist. Your clinic welcomes various clients, each arriving with severe skin conditions such as acne, dark circles, unplucked eyebrows, and dirty faces. It is your job to transform them! The game is divided into detailed, step-by-step processes. First, you will perform deep skincare treatments: popping pimples, applying soothing masks, trimming eyebrows, and cleansing the skin. Once their face is a flawless canvas, you unleash your creativity in the makeup phase. Choose from a wide array of colorful eyeshadows, eyeliners, blushes, lipsticks, and contact lenses to create a stunning look. Finally, complete the ultimate makeover by selecting fashionable hairstyles, dresses, and jewelry.
