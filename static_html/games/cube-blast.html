About Toy Blast

Toy Blast is a vibrant and highly addictive casual puzzle game that brings endless joy to players of all ages. Set in a charming, candy-colored world, the game challenges you to clear colorful cubes by tapping matching adjacent blocks. Unlike traditional swipe-to-match games, here you just tap to blast! Each level comes with unique goals, such as collecting a specific number of colored blocks or breaking through obstacles like ice. As you progress, you can combine larger groups of cubes to create spectacular power-ups like Rockets, Bombs, and Disco Balls to clear the board with a bang. With its limited moves system, you'll need a bit of strategy to pass the tricky levels. Featuring beautiful 2D cartoon graphics, hundreds of progressively challenging stages, and a relaxing yet engaging gameplay loop, Toy Blast is the perfect game to unwind and test your puzzle-solving skills. To begin, simply tap the screen when the swinging claw is aimed at a target you want to retrieve. The claw will extend and grab whatever it hits first. Larger items like big gold nuggets are worth more but take longer to pull up. Diamonds are small and fast but extremely valuable. If you accidentally grab a heavy rock, you can use a dynamite stick (if you have one) to destroy it and save time.
