About Fire Blob Winter

Fireblob Winter is a charming 2D pixel-art platformer that combines traditional platforming action with a unique melting mechanic. You control a glowing little fireblob wearing a festive Santa hat, on a mission to light up a series of campfires in a freezing winter landscape. The catch? Your very presence melts the icy world around you. As you move and jump across slippery platforms, certain ice blocks will vanish the moment you touch them, forcing you to think quickly and plan your route with care. Each level presents a new set of environmental puzzles, requiring precise jumps and strategic movement to reach the goal. With its cozy retro aesthetic, smooth physics-based movement, and two distinct game modes—Standard and Speedrun—Fireblob Winter offers a delightful challenge for both casual players and competitive runners.
