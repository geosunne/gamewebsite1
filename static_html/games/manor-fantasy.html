About Manor Fantasy

Welcome to Manor Fantasy, a delightful and relaxing casual game that perfectly combines merge-2 puzzle mechanics with creative home decoration. In this heartwarming story, you help two sisters earn money by making and selling delicious pastries to fund the renovation of their granny's rundown manor. You will start with a board full of basic ingredients and tools. By merging two identical items, you create higher-level goods like cakes, pies, and coffee. Fulfill customer orders to earn green stars, which are then used to complete various renovation tasks, from fixing floors to choosing stylish furniture. With its charming cartoon graphics, engaging storyline, and stress-relieving gameplay, Manor Fantasy offers endless hours of cozy entertainment. Uncover the secrets of the old house and design your dream manor step by step! Your main goal is to collect all the coins in each level and reach the goal without being caught by the police fish. When the level starts, analyze the position of the coins and the movement patterns of the blue police guards. Touch and hold your character, then drag your finger across the screen to draw a line that passes through every coin and leads to the finish. Be careful: you must clear each level in a single movement to achieve a 3-star rating! In later stages, you must time your path perfectly to avoid moving barriers and walls.
