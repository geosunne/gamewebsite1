About Super Bounce

Super Bounce is an intensely addictive hyper-casual game that will test your precision and sense of timing. The premise is beautifully simple: guide a glowing ball to jump continuously from one pillar to the next. The distance between the platforms varies constantly, requiring you to carefully judge how much power to put into each jump. By pressing and holding the screen, you charge the ball's jump meter; releasing your finger launches the ball forward. It is a true test of skill—jump too far or too short, and your ball will fall into the abyss, ending your run. Hitting the exact center of a platform rewards you with combo multipliers and bonus points, lighting up the screen with satisfying visual effects. With its minimalist 3D design, soothing yet challenging gameplay, and a built-in leaderboard system, Super Bounce offers endless replayability for players trying to beat their personal best scores.
