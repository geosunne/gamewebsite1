About Kill.io

Welcome to Kill.io, a fast-paced and incredibly fun top-down arena battle game where survival of the fittest is the only rule! In this quirky io-style action game, you control hilarious floating heads—ranging from cute pandas and classical heroes to funny internet memes—armed with bizarre melee weapons. The objective is simple: navigate the battlefield to collect shiny gems and treats to grow stronger, and eliminate your opponents to climb the leaderboard. The intuitive \\\"drag to move, release to attack\\\" control scheme makes it easy for anyone to pick up and play, yet offers surprisingly deep tactical combat. Outmaneuver your foes, strike when they least expect it, and dominate the arena. With a wide variety of wacky skins to unlock, daily rewards, and action-packed short matches, Kill.io is the perfect game to relieve stress and prove your competitive skills!
