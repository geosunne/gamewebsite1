About Mahjong Link Puzzle

Mahjong Link Puzzle takes the classic tile-matching genre and adds a fresh, brain-teasing twist. Unlike traditional connection games, this unique puzzle requires players to clear the board by aligning two identical Mahjong tiles on the exact same straight line—either horizontally or vertically—with no other obstacles blocking the path between them. To achieve this, you must strategically drag and slide tiles across the board into available empty spaces. This innovative sliding mechanic transforms the game into a dynamic spatial puzzle that truly tests your logic and forward-thinking. Set against relaxing oriental backgrounds with a ticking timer to keep you on your toes, the game offers a perfect blend of zen aesthetics and stimulating mental challenges.
