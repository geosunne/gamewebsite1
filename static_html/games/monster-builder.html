About Monster Builder

Welcome to the bizarre and hilarious world of Monster Builder! In this unique 3D casual game, you play as a mad scientist tasked with creating the ultimate fighting champion. But you won't be using high-tech robots; instead, you'll be splicing together the body parts of various animals and mythical creatures! Start with a base body, like a sleek puma or a bulky elephant, and then carefully select a head, legs, and even a tail to complete your mutant masterpiece. Each part contributes to your monster's overall health and attack power. Once your bizarre creation is complete, send it into the snowy arena to battle against equally weird and wonderful AI opponents. With simple selection mechanics, funny animations, and a wide variety of parts to unlock, Monster Builder offers a ridiculously fun and creative experience. Build, fight, and discover the most powerful (and silliest) combinations!
