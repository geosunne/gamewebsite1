About SwordMaster

SwordMaster is an exhilarating and visually striking 3D runner game that redefines the action genre. You take control of a high-tech robotic warrior sprinting along a vibrant track. What sets this game apart is the unique weapon mechanic: a ring of razor-sharp spinning swords that orbit your character. Your primary objective is to navigate through mathematical \\\"Multiplier Gates\\\" that dynamically increase or decrease the number of swords in your arsenal. The more swords you have, the more devastating your attacks become! You'll face waves of enemies, obstacles, and powerful giant bosses that require a high volume of blades to defeat. With its smooth mechanics, satisfying slicing effects, and addictive progression loop, SwordMaster offers a perfect blend of quick reflexes and strategic planning for players looking for intense, casual fun.
