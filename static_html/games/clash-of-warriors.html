You will collect and deploy a variety of hero cards, from sturdy knights and pitchfork-wielding peasants to magical goblins. Each card has its own attack power and health points. The key to victory lies in how you place your heroes on the board to attack the opponent's forces while defending your own. Win matches in the Arena to earn coins, unlock new card packs (Silver, Golden, Diamond), and upgrade your existing deck to unleash even greater power.
