About Slices

Welcome to Slices, a highly addictive and fast-paced puzzle game that will put your spatial logic and quick thinking to the ultimate test! In this deliciously fun hyper-casual game, your goal is to complete circular pies using various slices of food, ranging from vibrant fruits like watermelons and kiwis to tasty pizza slices. A random slice configuration appears in the center of the screen, and you must tap one of the six surrounding outer circles to place it. Once an outer circle is completely filled with six slices, it pops and disappears, rewarding you with points and freeing up valuable space. But be careful! If you try to place a slice into a circle that is already occupied in that specific angle, you will run out of space and the game is over. Plan your moves ahead, trigger massive combos, and beat your high score!
