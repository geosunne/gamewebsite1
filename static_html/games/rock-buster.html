About Rock Buster

Rock Buster is an exhilarating hyper-casual shooting game that blends simple arcade mechanics with strategic progression. Players take control of a nimble mini-tank navigating a vast field filled with numbered rock obstacles. Your primary objective is to blast your way through these blocks by firing continuous projectiles. Each rock displays a number indicating how many hits it requires to shatter. As you progress, the numbers increase, demanding better reflexes and stronger firepower. Between levels, you can use your hard-earned coins to upgrade your tank's power and income rate, ensuring you stay ahead of the rising difficulty.
