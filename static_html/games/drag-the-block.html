About Drag The Block

Welcome to Drag The Block, a highly addictive and stress-relieving puzzle game that puts a fresh spin on classic block elimination! In this vibrant 2D game, your goal is to slide colorful, cute monster blocks left or right. If there is empty space beneath them, they will drop down. By strategically dropping blocks into gaps, you must create full horizontal lines to clear them from the board. But beware! After every move, a new row of blocks pushes up from the bottom, bringing your stack closer to the terrifying monster's mouth at the top of the screen. If any block touches the top, it is game over! Utilize your spatial logic, trigger massive combo clears, and use powerful items like the Hammer and Color Fairy to survive as long as possible. Perfect for a quick mental workout, Drag The Block is easy to learn but hard to master! Endless Exploration: Dig through infinite underground levels to discover rarer and more valuable resources.\ \ Idle Earnings: Your mining operation continues to generate profits even when the game is closed.\ \ Retro Pixel Art: Immerse yourself in a beautifully crafted 2D pixel-art environment with a nostalgic vibe.\ \ Strategic Upgrades: Invest your gold wisely in better pickaxes, faster elevators, and automated machinery.\ \ Massive Scale: Watch your small hole in the ground expand into a multi-level mining complex. Start your journey by clicking on the ground to help your miner dig and collect basic ores. Once you gather enough resources, move them to the surface to sell for gold. Use your hard-earned money at the shop to purchase your first set of upgrades, such as a sharper pickaxe for faster digging or increased carrying capacity. As you progress, you can build ladders to reach deeper layers where high-value gems like rubies and emeralds are found. The key to success is automation: hire managers to oversee different shafts and upgrade the central elevator and warehouse to handle the increasing flow of resources. Balance your spending between depth and efficiency to maximize your hourly profit and become the ultimate mining tycoon! Use your Mouse Click or Tap the screen to interact with menus, upgrade buttons, and your miner.
