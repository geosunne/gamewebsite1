About Stone Line

Stone Line is a visually vibrant and highly engaging casual puzzle game designed to help you relax and test your pattern-recognition skills. The core gameplay revolves around connecting identically colored stones to clear them from the board and rack up massive points. Unlike traditional match-3 games where you swap adjacent items, Stone Line allows you to draw continuous lines across neighboring gems—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—giving you more freedom and strategic depth. With its intuitive mechanics, colorful 2D graphics, and satisfying visual effects upon clearing large clusters, the game offers a perfect blend of relaxation and mental stimulation. Whether you have just a few minutes to spare or want to immerse yourself in a long session of gem-matching fun, Stone Line provides an endlessly entertaining experience that progressively challenges your ability to find the longest possible connections.
