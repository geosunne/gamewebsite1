About Find Bath Water

Find Bath Water is a charming and humorous puzzle game that puts your logical thinking to the test. The game follows a girl who is ready for her bath, only to find that the plumbing is completely disconnected! As a helpful handyman, your task is to manipulate flexible green pipes to create a continuous path for the water to flow from the reservoir to the showerhead. The game features vibrant 2D cartoon graphics and hilarious character expressions that add a layer of comedy to every level. As you progress, the pipe networks become increasingly complex, requiring you to think several steps ahead to avoid dead ends or wasting precious water. With its simple mechanics and progressively challenging puzzles, Find Bath Water offers a satisfying and relaxing experience for players of all ages who enjoy a good brain workout.
