About Sword Master Flash

Sword Master Flash is an exhilarating and fast-paced 2D action game that combines tactical precision with high-speed ninja slicing. You play as a squishy but incredibly skilled jelly swordmaster navigating through dangerous aerial corridors filled with armed enemies and deadly traps. The core mechanic is unique: you don't just run; you launch yourself through the air like a flash of lightning. By dragging on the screen, you control the trajectory of your jelly ninja's dash. Release to fly forward, slicing through every opponent in your path with satisfying visual feedback. As you progress, the levels become more complex, requiring you to chain multiple dashes to avoid spikes and reach the finish line. With its clean cartoon aesthetics, rewarding \\\"mowing\\\" combat, and unlockable rare swords, Sword Master Flash offers a perfect blend of strategy and adrenaline-fueled fun.
