About Miner Rush

Welcome to Miner Rush, an exciting and fast-paced hypercasual arcade game where your goal is to dig as deep as possible! In this addictive endless runner-style game, you take control of a quirky, one-eyed drilling machine. Your mission is to descend through layers of earth and stone, collecting precious gold nuggets along the way. The gameplay is simple yet incredibly challenging: the drill moves horizontally across the screen, and you must tap to make it dig downwards. Timing is everything! If you mistime your tap and the drill hits the edge of the screen, your run is over. With its vibrant 2D cartoon graphics and straightforward mechanics, Miner Rush is perfect for quick gaming sessions. Test your reflexes, gather gold to increase your score, and see if you can top the leaderboards. Can you handle the rush and become the ultimate deep-earth miner?
