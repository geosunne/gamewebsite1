About Big Head

Big Head is an exhilarating and fast-paced 3D runner game that combines charming voxel aesthetics with challenging platforming mechanics. Players take control of a unique character characterized by their oversized head, navigating through an ever-changing environment filled with treacherous gaps and obstacles. The game emphasizes timing and precision; one wrong move could send your big-headed hero tumbling into the void. As you sprint across the platforms, you'll need to collect golden coins to boost your score and potentially unlock rewards.
