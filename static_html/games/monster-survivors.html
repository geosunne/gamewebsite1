How to play Monster Survivors

Press Play, wait for the browser game to load, then follow the on-screen prompts. Focus on the main objective, learn each restart, and use the related games list when you want another survival game.

Controls

WASD: Move your character. Mouse: Aim your weapon. Left Click: Fire weapon. Space: Special ability. E: Interact / Pick up. ESC: Pause menu.
