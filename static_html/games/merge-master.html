About Merge Master

Merge Master is a highly satisfying and relaxing 3D sorting puzzle game that brings the joy of organizing a messy supermarket right to your screen. Instead of a traditional flat board, players interact with realistic multi-tier shelves stocked with a chaotic mix of everyday 3D groceries, snacks, and drinks. Your objective is to meticulously sort these items. By dragging a product from one shelf to another, you aim to align three identical items next to each other on the same shelf. Once three matching goods are grouped, they instantly vanish, freeing up valuable shelf space! As you progress through increasingly cluttered levels, you must use strategic thinking and sharp observation to clear the shelves efficiently. With its vibrant 3D models and therapeutic gameplay, Merge Master is the ultimate brain-teaser for puzzle and organization lovers alike.
