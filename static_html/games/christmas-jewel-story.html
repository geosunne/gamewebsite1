Your objective is to move all nuts of the same color onto individual screws. To start, tap on any screw to \\\"lift\\\" the top-most nut. Then, tap another screw to place that nut onto it. When a screw is filled with four matching colors, the nuts will be \\\"collected\\\" with a celebratory effect.
