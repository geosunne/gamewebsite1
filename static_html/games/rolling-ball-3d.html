Rolling Ball 3D is a pulse-pounding arcade runner that challenges your focus, precision, and reaction time. In this visually stunning 3D world, you take control of a marble racing across narrow wooden tracks suspended high in the air. The concept is simple but the execution is demanding: keep the ball on the path at all costs. As the speed increases, you must navigate sharp turns, steep drops, and unexpected obstacles that threaten to send you plummeting into the abyss. Every level features a unique environment, from lush forests to futuristic cityscapes. Along the way, you can collect gems and coins to unlock a variety of themed ball skins, such as basketballs or metallic orbs.
