About Stack Ball

Stack Ball is an exhilarating 3D hyper-casual arcade game where players smash, bump, and bounce their way through revolving helix platforms to reach the end. The premise is simple but highly addictive: your ball acts like a wrecking ball, smashing through the brightly colored platforms that block its descent. However, you must carefully avoid the solid black blocks! If your ball hits a black area while smashing, it shatters into pieces, and you fail the level. But there is a thrilling twist—if you continuously smash through platforms without stopping, your ball catches on fire. In this blazing state, it becomes completely invincible and can even destroy the dreaded black blocks. With its vibrant graphics and satisfying destruction, Stack Ball offers a perfect stress-relieving experience.
