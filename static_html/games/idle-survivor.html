About Idle Survivor

Welcome to Idle Survivor, a relaxing and highly addictive 3D voxel-style idle game where your journey begins with nothing but a tiny raft in the middle of a vast, uncharted ocean. Your mission is to survive, build, and explore! Starting with a single piece of floating wood, you must gather drifting resources from the sea to expand your wooden sanctuary. Construct new planks, plant carrot fields for sustainable food, light torches for the night, and gradually build a massive floating base. As you sail across the deserted sea, you can check your map to discover new ocean areas and rescue lost survivors who will join your crew. With its charming blocky graphics reminiscent of classic sandbox games, offline progression, and simple tapping mechanics, Idle Survivor offers endless hours of incremental building fun.
