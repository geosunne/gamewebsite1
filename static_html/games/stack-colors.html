About Stack Colors

Stack Colors is an exhilarating and vibrant 3D hyper-casual runner game that tests your reflexes and color recognition skills. You control a dynamic character running along an endless track filled with colorful tiles. The goal is simple: collect the blocks that match your character's current color to build a massive stack while carefully avoiding blocks of any other color. As you progress, you will pass through glowing portals that instantly change your character's color, requiring quick thinking and rapid adjustments. The bigger your stack, the better! At the end of each level, you tap rapidly to build up speed and deliver a powerful kick to your stacked blocks, sending them flying across score multipliers. With its smooth gameplay, colorful graphics, and satisfying mechanics, Stack Colors offers an incredibly addictive and stress-relieving gaming experience.
