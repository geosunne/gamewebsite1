About Monkey Tycoon

Welcome to Monkey Tycoon, a fun and addictive supermarket management game where you play as an adorable, hardworking monkey entrepreneur! Your journey begins with a small fruit stand, planting banana trees and stocking your very first wooden shelf. As animal customers arrive to buy your fresh produce, you will earn cash to continuously expand your business. What starts as a simple setup quickly evolves into a bustling mini-market. You will soon unlock new farming plots for corn, which you can use to feed chickens to produce fresh eggs, or feed cows to get milk. As your store grows, you can hire monkey assistants to help restock shelves and handle the busy cash register. With its charming 2D cartoon graphics, intuitive idle-arcade mechanics, and deeply satisfying progression, Monkey Mart offers endless hours of tycoon fun. Build your empire today!
