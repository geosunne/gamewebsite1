About WC Waiting Line

WC Waiting Line is a clever and addictive 2D puzzle game that puts your logic and color-matching skills to the test in a hilarious everyday scenario. The game features a crowded line of people waiting for the restroom, each wearing a specific color. Your goal is to tap them into the available toilet stalls at the top. However, there's a catch: a character will only leave the stall (be cleared) if their color matches the toilet paper currently assigned to that stall. With limited toilet slots available, you must carefully plan the order of your taps. If all the stalls are filled with characters who don't match the paper colors, the line gets stuck and the game is over! It's a perfect blend of spatial management and color matching that offers a satisfyingly \\\"Zen\\\" experience as you clear the chaotic crowd.
