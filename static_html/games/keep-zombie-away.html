Welcome to Keep Zombie Away, a thrilling and strategic tower defense survival game where your bed is your best weapon! Set in a creepy, dimly lit school dormitory, you and other players must survive the night against a terrifying, relentless zombie. As the countdown begins, you must quickly scramble to find an empty room, close the door, and jump into bed. Once asleep, you will start generating coins automatically. Use this passive income wisely to upgrade your wooden door, build defensive turrets, and construct generators to produce energy for advanced technologies. But beware—the zombie will randomly attack doors to break in and eliminate players. You must constantly balance repairing your door with upgrading your weapons to fend off the undead menace. Can you outsmart the monster, build the ultimate fortress, and survive until dawn? Join the suspenseful adventure in Keep Zombie Away today!
