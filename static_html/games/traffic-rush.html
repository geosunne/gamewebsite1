About Traffic Rush

Traffic Rush is a thrilling and highly addictive hyper-casual driving game that will put your timing and reflexes to the ultimate test. Viewed from a top-down perspective, you control a car navigating through bustling city streets filled with dangerous intersections. The gameplay is simple yet incredibly challenging: your car moves forward automatically, but you must tap and hold the screen to speed up and cross intersections, or release your finger to stop and wait for cross-traffic to pass. You must carefully dodge fast-moving police cars, ambulances, and taxis that will not slow down for you! Along the way, collect stacks of green cash scattered on the road. Successfully cross the finish line to complete the level, and use your hard-earned money in the Shop to unlock a variety of awesome new vehicles, including sports cars and trucks.
