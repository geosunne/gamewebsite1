About Hotel Party

Welcome to Hotel Party, the most addictive and fast-paced idle management game! In this vibrant 3D world, you start as a dedicated hotel manager with a single goal: to build the most luxurious accommodation empire in town. The journey begins with a single humble room. You'll need to greet guests at the reception, provide them with their keys, and ensure their stay is comfortable. Once they check out, collect the stacks of cash they leave behind to fund your rapid growth. Every dollar earned can be reinvested into unlocking new room zones, upgrading furniture, or hiring specialized staff like cleaners to help you manage the increasing flow of visitors. As your reputation grows, you'll unlock more floors and complex facilities. With its colorful graphics, smooth animations, and intuitive gameplay, Hotel Party offers a highly satisfying mix of strategy and relaxation. Can you handle the pressure of being the boss and become the ultimate hotel tycoon? Your primary objective is to reach the target number shown at the top of the screen (e.g., \\\"next target: 10\\\"). To start, drag a numbered block from the selection area at the bottom onto an identical block in the grid. When two or more blocks with the same number touch, they will merge into a single block with a value increased by one (e.g., two '2's become one '3'). Plan your placements carefully to avoid filling the grid completely, as you lose if no more moves are possible. Higher-numbered blocks, indicated by crowns or cat paws, are more valuable.
