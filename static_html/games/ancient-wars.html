About Ancient Wars

Ancient Wars is a captivating 2D tug-of-war strategy game that takes you back to the dawn of civilization. In this addictive arcade-style adventure, you are the leader of a growing tribe tasked with expanding your territory and crushing rival forces. The core gameplay revolves around resource management and strategic unit deployment. You must carefully spend your food and gold to spawn a variety of warriors, starting from basic club-wielding cavemen to more advanced fighters. The battle takes place across a single lane, where your troops clash with waves of enemies coming from the opposite side. Your ultimate goal is to destroy the enemy base while protecting your own from being overrun. As you progress, you can upgrade your units' stats and your base's production speed. With its charming minimalist graphics and rewarding progression system, Ancient Wars offers endless hours of tactical fun for players of all ages. Can you lead your army to ultimate glory?
