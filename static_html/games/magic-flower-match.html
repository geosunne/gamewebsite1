About Magic Flower Match

\\\"Magic Flower Match\\\" is a charming and highly addictive puzzle game that brings a fresh, floral twist to the popular tile-matching genre. In this beautiful garden-themed game, you are presented with a pile of colorful flower tiles arranged in various creative shapes, like an apple or a butterfly. Your objective is simple: tap on the flowers to move them into the 7 empty slots at the top of the screen. Once you get three identical flowers in the slots, they will magically merge and be planted into a beautiful flower pot! However, you must be careful—the tiles are stacked on top of each other, and you can only select the ones on the uppermost layer. If your 7 slots fill up without making a match, the game is over. With its vibrant graphics, soothing gameplay, and increasingly complex puzzle layouts, \\\"Magic Flower Match\\\" is the perfect way to unwind and keep your mind sharp.
