About Water Sort

Water Sort is a captivating and addictive puzzle game that perfectly balances mental challenge with a relaxing atmosphere. The core objective is simple yet deeply engaging: players must sort the colored water in the glass tubes until all colors are separated into their own bottles. As you progress through thousands of levels, the difficulty increases, requiring more strategic planning and logical thinking. The game features a clean, minimalist 2D art style with smooth pouring animations that provide a highly satisfying and stress-relieving experience. Beyond the puzzles, players can earn coins to unlock a wide variety of themed backgrounds and unique bottle shapes, ranging from classic flasks to cute milk bottles and scientific tubes. Whether you are looking for a quick brain exercise or a long, soothing gaming session, Water Sort is the ideal choice for puzzle enthusiasts worldwide.
