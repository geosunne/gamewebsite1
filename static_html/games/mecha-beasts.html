About Mecha Beasts

Welcome to Mecha Beasts, an action-packed, turn-based RPG where futuristic technology and primal instincts collide! In this thrilling game, you step into the role of a brilliant mecha commander tasked with assembling and upgrading a squad of powerful mechanical warriors. Enter the \\\"Mecha Depot\\\" to enhance your robot's limbs, armor, and core stats, preparing them for intense 1v1 battles in the Star Battle arena. During combat, choose your skills strategically to outmaneuver opponent mechas. The highlight of the game is the spectacular transformation mechanic—unleash your ultimate power to turn your humanoid robot into a devastating Mecha T-Rex, wiping out enemies with massive damage. With stunning 2D anime-style graphics, deep progression systems, and endless robotic opponents to conquer, Mecha Beasts delivers an epic fighting experience for all sci-fi fans.
