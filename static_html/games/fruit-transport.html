About Fruit Transport

Step into the driver's seat with Fruit Transport, a satisfying sorting and logistics puzzle game. Your mission is to manage a busy fruit warehouse by organizing mixed crates of produce. Drag and drop fruit slices from storage trays onto temporary holding areas or directly into delivery trucks below. The goal is to sort identical fruits—like apples, oranges, grapes, and strawberries—so that each truck carries only one type of fruit. Once a truck is full, it departs, clearing space and earning you points. With increasing difficulty levels and a ticking clock, you'll need quick thinking and strategic planning to keep the supply chain moving. Featuring colorful graphics and intuitive drag-and-drop controls, Fruit Transport offers a relaxing yet challenging experience perfect for puzzle lovers. Can you become the ultimate fruit logistics manager? Start sorting today!
