About Slice It All

Welcome to the highly satisfying and relaxing world of Slice It All! This addictive hyper-casual game puts you in control of a sharp knife on a thrilling journey to slice through a variety of objects. Your main goal is to keep the knife moving forward by tapping the screen to make it flip and jump. As you progress through the vibrant 3D environments, you will effortlessly cut through fruits, colored pencils, wooden blocks, and other fun obstacles, triggering incredibly satisfying visual and physical effects. The game features intuitive one-tap controls, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play anytime. However, proper timing is key to avoid falling into the void. With endless levels and a rewarding score multiplier pillar at the end of each stage, Slice It All is the perfect stress-relieving experience. Jump in and slice your way to victory! Welcome to the bizarre and hilarious world of Monster Builder! In this unique 3D casual game, you play as a mad scientist tasked with creating the ultimate fighting champion.
