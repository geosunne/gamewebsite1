About Blackhole Hero

Black Hole Hero is an incredibly satisfying 3D arcade game that puts you in control of a cosmic vacuum. Your mission is simple yet addictive: move your black hole across the map to swallow various items, primarily high-caliber ammunition and powerful weapons. The more you consume, the larger your black hole becomes, allowing you to devour even bigger objects. This game perfectly captures the \\\"swallow-to-grow\\\" mechanic seen in popular .io titles but adds a strategic layer with timed challenges and persistent upgrades. With its clean 3D graphics and smooth physics, every item sucked into the void provides a sense of relaxation and accomplishment. As you progress, you'll need to manage your time wisely and prioritize the right items to ensure you're powerful enough to complete each level's objective.
