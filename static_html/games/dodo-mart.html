About Dodo Mart

Welcome to DodoMart, the most satisfying goods sorting and triple match puzzle game! Have you ever dreamed of organizing a supermarket perfectly? Now you can! In DodoMart, you are faced with shelves filled with various items, from tasty snacks like chips and soda to cute plushies and daily necessities. Your goal is to drag and drop items to ensure that three identical objects are placed on the same shelf segment. Once matched, they will vanish with a satisfying effect, earning you points and clearing space for more sorting. The game features a variety of challenging levels with a 3-minute time limit, pushing your observation and speed to the limit. With smooth 2D animations, vibrant colors, and helpful boosters like the \\\"Eliminate Hammer,\\\" DodoMart provides an addictive experience that is both mentally stimulating and incredibly relaxing. Start your tidying journey now and clear every shelf in the mart! Use this cash to upgrade your base level at the central hub, which unlocks more advanced buildings like Milk Shelves or larger plots of land.
