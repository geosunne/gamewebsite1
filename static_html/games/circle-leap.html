About Circle Leap

\\\"Circle Leap\\\" is a pure, minimalist hyper-casual game designed to test your reflexes and timing. With its clean, distraction-free interface and simple one-tap mechanics, the game offers an instantly engaging experience. Your goal is to guide a small circle as it leaps from one rotating larger ring to the next. The challenge lies in finding the perfect moment to tap—jump too early or too late, and you might miss the next ring or crash into a jagged, star-shaped obstacle! As you successfully jump from ring to ring, your score increases, and the background color shifts, keeping the visual experience fresh. Whether you are looking to kill a few minutes while waiting or want to challenge yourself to beat your personal best, \\\"Circle Leap\\\" is the perfect addictive arcade game for you.
