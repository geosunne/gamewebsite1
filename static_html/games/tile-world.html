Welcome to Tile World, the ultimate 3D match puzzle game designed to test your observation skills and help you relax! In this highly addictive brain teaser, your goal is to clear a massive pile of colorful 3D objects scattered on the screen. From delicious food like burgers and donuts to everyday items like cameras and toy cars, the variety is endless. To play, simply tap an object to move it into the collection bar at the bottom. When you gather three identical items in the bar, they will be matched and cleared! However, you must be strategic: the collection bar only has 7 slots. If it fills up completely without any matches, the game is over.
