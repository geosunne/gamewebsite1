About Star Plane

Star Plane is an exhilarating top-down space shooter that seamlessly blends classic arcade action with engaging roguelike mechanics. Set in a beautifully vibrant sci-fi universe, you pilot an advanced spacecraft tasked with surviving endless waves of cosmic debris and hostile forces. Your ship fires automatically, allowing you to focus entirely on maneuvering through dense asteroid fields and dodging incoming attacks. What makes Star Plane truly addictive is its dynamic progression system. Every time you clear a wave or level up, you get to choose from three randomized skills—such as scattered bullets, critical rate boosts, or weapon enhancements—creating a unique combat build for every run. Between battles, you can use the wealth you've collected to permanently upgrade your ship's attack power and fire rate.
