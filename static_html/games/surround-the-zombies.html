About Surround The Zombies

Welcome to Surround The Zombies, an immersive strategy tower defense game where your tactical skills mean the difference between life and death! In a world overrun by the undead and terrifying monsters, your mission is to build, upgrade, and manage the ultimate survivor base. Expand your population capacity, unlock powerful troop buildings, and train specialized soldiers to fight off relentless enemy waves. What sets this game apart is its dynamic defense system: you can freely move your buildings and defensive structures to counter zombie attacks coming from multiple directions. Upgrade your main headquarters to unlock new technologies, enhance your units' star levels for massive damage, and complete daily quests to earn valuable rewards from the shop. With deep strategic planning and base management, Surround The Zombies offers a thrilling survival experience. Can you build an impenetrable fortress? The goal of Halloween Mahjong Connect is to remove all tiles from the board by matching pairs of identical images. To match a pair, click on two identical tiles that are either adjacent or can be connected by a line that turns no more than twice. If the path between two tiles is blocked by other tiles, they cannot be matched. Pay close attention to the timer at the bottom of the screen; every successful match adds a bit of time back. If you get stuck, click the lightbulb icon for a hint or the pause button to take a break. Clear the entire grid to advance to the next level and achieve a high score! Use the mouse left-click or tap the screen to select and match tiles.
