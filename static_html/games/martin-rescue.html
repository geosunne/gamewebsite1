About Martin Rescue

Martin Rescue is a hilarious and brain-teasing logic puzzle game that takes the highly popular \\\"pull the pin\\\" mechanic on a comical adventure. Players must help Martin, a sharp-dressed but highly unfortunate guy, navigate through a variety of tricky scenarios. Unlike simple dropping games, Martin actively walks forward to reach his specific goal for the level—whether it's escaping a dangerous room, finding a restroom, or reaching an item. However, his path is always blocked by golden pins and diverse, level-specific hazards like aggressive dogs, thugs, lava pits, or spikes. Your job is to use your IQ and logical thinking to pull the pins in the correct sequence. The right move might drop a heavy boulder onto an enemy or use water to extinguish a fire, clearing a safe path for Martin to walk through.
