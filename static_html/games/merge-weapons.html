Merge Weapons is a highly satisfying and relaxing casual game that perfectly blends the popular \\\"merge-2\\\" mechanics with a physics-based, arcade-style dropping phase. The real fun begins when you release these upgraded weapons into the dropping zone! Watch as your swords fall, bounce, and smash through obstacles and lower-tier items on their path downward. Every successful hit generates gold coins, which you can then use to buy even more weapons to continue your merging cycle.
