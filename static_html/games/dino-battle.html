About Dino Battle

Dino Battle is an engaging 3D strategic merge game set in a prehistoric era where humans and dinosaurs fight side by side. Your objective is simple yet challenging: build a powerful army to crush the enemy tribes. The game utilizes a popular merge mechanic where you purchase basic units—primitive archers and various dinosaur species—and place them on a tactical grid. By combining two identical units, you can evolve them into stronger versions with significantly higher health and damage output. With vibrant cartoonish graphics and intuitive gameplay, every level presents a new tactical puzzle. You must decide whether to expand your numbers or focus on high-level elite units to win. As you progress, the enemies become tougher, requiring smarter placement and faster evolution. It's a perfect blend of casual puzzle-solving and auto-battle excitement that keeps you coming back for more.
