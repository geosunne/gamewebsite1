Each character provides a unique set of skills and weapons designed to drain your opponents' HP. Whether you prefer dropping explosive mines in your wake or activating high-energy shields to absorb damage, your tactical choices are key. Once inside the circular arena, it’s a fight to the death. Outmaneuver your rivals, land heavy hits, and watch their health bars deplete. Every enemy destroyed brings you one step closer to unlocking the most powerful character icons in the game!
